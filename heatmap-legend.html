Heatmaps provide visual insights into warehouse operations by overlaying data-driven colors on storage locations.

  • Darker/More Intense Colors: Higher activity, volume, or issues
  • Lighter/Cooler Colors: Lower activity or normal conditions
  • No Color/Transparent: No activity or data for that location
